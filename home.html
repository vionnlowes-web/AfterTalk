Our Research

Our research examines how the development of ChatGPT affects senior high APEC Schools students and teachers. We analyze the positive and negative traits of its influence on the education system for the APEC Schools community. We also wish to interact and discuss with our audience using entertaining content and providing forms in order to look further to our research with their help!
